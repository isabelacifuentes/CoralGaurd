Purpose

Signal Safe is a revolutionary AI-based initiative aimed at improving road safety. Our mission is to prevent traffic accidents caused by red-light violations using cutting-edge AI technology that detects and classifies traffic light states in real time.

Who We Are

We are a team of innovators, engineers, and road safety advocates dedicated to leveraging artificial intelligence to solve real-world problems. Our team combines expertise in machine learning, data science, and urban traffic management to create a safer driving environment for everyone.

What We Will Do

Signal Safe plans to deploy a smart camera system that can detect traffic light states and provide real-time alerts to drivers. Our roadmap includes:

  • Developing and testing our AI model with traffic light data.
  • Collaborating with city planners and traffic authorities to integrate the system.
  • Ensuring sustainability by introducing energy-efficient hardware.
  • Exploring AI integration with self-driving vehicles.

Learn more about our roadmap in our presentation.
